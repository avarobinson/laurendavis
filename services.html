Individual Therapy

Individual therapy can provide an experience allowing for emotional growth and change. Through determining each person’s unique treatment needs, frequency of sessions and length of treatment are determined. Some people approach therapy wanting help with an urgent problem while others are hoping for exploration that goes “beneath the surface”. Other times, it’s some combination of the two.

Some issues that people seek individual therapy for include but are not limited to:

  • Low self esteem
  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Ending or starting a relationship
  • Loss and grief
  • Difficulties in work or social life
  • Traumatic experience
  • Difficulty with parenting
