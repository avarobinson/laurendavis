Lauren B. Davis

LCSW

As a psychotherapist and clinical social worker, I work with people across the lifespan in a psychodynamic, insight-oriented way. I bring experience in community mental health as well as ongoing training in treating issues related to self-esteem, childhood trauma, relationships, loss, life transitions and parenting.

I strive to create a space where therapeutic trust can be experienced. In this space, I will listen closely to your lived experience and history to try to understand what is getting in the way of living fully and authentically.

Sometimes, people have had experiences in their past that continue to impact them. Others have difficulty in relationships with peers, with family members or in their professional lives. I help people identify the “root cause” of these difficulties and find ways to understand their meaning while building upon already existing strengths to live more satisfyingly in the world.
